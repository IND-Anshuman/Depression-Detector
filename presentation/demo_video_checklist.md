# 5-Minute Demo Checklist

1. Show the disclaimer banner and privacy note first.
2. Start the webcam stream and point out the holistic overlay.
3. Explain the risk gauge, 3-class bars, and continuous score.
4. Highlight modality contribution bars and attention heatmap.
5. Show FPS / latency readouts while moving closer and farther from the camera.
6. Mention the manifest-first training path and reproducibility tooling.
7. End with limitations and the non-diagnostic nature of the system.
